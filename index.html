Voice of Women Foundation

Voice of Women Foundation is an NGO (80G tax exemption applied) aimed at igniting the social and conscience and overall development of the people especially women and children, while empowering them with the life skills and employment.

Over the last 4 years, VWF has been involving with people of Delhi/NCR for regular and consistent social action programmes.

What do we do

MEDICAL

  • Free eye check-up and free cataract operation
  • Free Cancer detection and awareness
  • Free full-body health check-ups (BP, sugar, ECG, bone density)
  • Awareness on HIV & TB
  • Dental Check-ups
  • Blood donation camp

CHILDREN

  • Masti KI Paathshala : Life-skill training
  • Academic Support
  • Free English Speaking Classes
  • Donation Drives: Shoes, books, stationary, clothes, sweater
  • Awareness program on child abuse with Delhi Police

WOMEN

  • Legal awareness of women right
  • Awareness on Menstrual hygiene
  • Employment generation: making of Achaar, Papad, Masks, Bags, Handmade Soap, Agarbatti, Dhoopbatti
  • Organizing marriage of less-privileged girls
  • Self Defence Training with DELHI POLICE for young girls
